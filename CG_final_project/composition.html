Composition


To give a general and unified overview of the models created as part of this final project, was created a composition that shows an example of furniture through the proper assembly of the different models. The code that generates this composition contains, without repetition of functions or domain's variables, the codes of all the models that were then translated, rotated and multiplied in order to obtain the result shown below.
